How to read these

▦ Shared provenance convention

In every review, citation chips follow each sourced claim and link to that review's Corpus page, which links onward to arXiv or the publishing lab — the chain is always claim → chip → bibliography → source. Claim-type tags (Empirical Theory Proposal Definition) show how strong a claim is in its source. Amber callouts are the author's inference, never a paper's claim; blue callouts are paper findings; teal blockquotes are verbatim. Each review is a sealed reading of its own corpus — they are not merged.

Each topic is fully self-contained under its own path; use the “↑ All SotA” link in any review's nav to return here.
